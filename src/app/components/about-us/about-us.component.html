About New Fully Bakery

Bringing you the finest baked goods since 1986

Our Story

New Fully Bakery was founded in 1986 with a simple mission: to bring the traditional art of baking to modern communities. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved local institution, serving fresh, handcrafted baked goods to thousands of satisfied customers.

Our founder, Master Baker Luis Hsieh, brings over 50 years of experience from traditional Taiwanese bakeries. Combined with modern techniques and the finest ingredients, we create products that honor both tradition and innovation.
